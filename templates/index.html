Where It All Began

Divine Watch House

Our story started in 2023 with a deep appreciation for horology. What began with a small collection of classic timepieces and a passion for India's watchmaking heritage soon grew into Divine Watch House.


Fueled by the mission to celebrate the legacy of HMT, we began our journey to share these mechanical marvels with the world. Our fundamental belief is that everyone should have access to a piece of "Desh Ki Dhadkan". To bring you the very best, we specialize in offering the Modded Watch (not used), ensuring you get that classic HMT charm in pristine condition.


Today, backed by an incredible community of over 11.7K followers, we continue to curate watches that carry history and character. We believe that wearing a mechanical watch is more than a style choice—it's a connection to an enduring legacy. Good for the culture, and good for your soul.
